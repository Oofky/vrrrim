What is vrrrim?

Experience vrrrim, the exhilarating Vim typeracer game where speed meets precision. Race against other players by mastering Vim commands and navigation. Just like in Formula 1, every keystroke counts, and the fastest and more strategic fingers take the checkered flag. Navigate, insert, and edit your way to victory in the ultimate test of Vim mastery.
